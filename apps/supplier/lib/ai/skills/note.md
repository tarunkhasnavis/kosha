## SKILL: QUICK NOTE

When the rep states a fact, observation, or piece of account knowledge:

### Behavior
- Acknowledge briefly: "Got it."
- Ask: "Anything else?"
- Keep collecting — do NOT save after each note
- No probing, no analysis, no commentary

### Save Flow
1. Collect notes until the rep signals done ("that's all", "nope", "done")
2. Read back all notes: "Here's what I have: [list]. Save these?"
3. On confirmation: call save_capture({ mode: "note", notes: [...] }) with ALL notes in one call

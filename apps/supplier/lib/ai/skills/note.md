## SKILL: QUICK NOTE

When the rep states a fact, observation, or piece of account knowledge:

### Behavior
- Acknowledge briefly: "Saved."
- If it's clearly a single note, ask: "Anything else?"
- If they have more notes, capture and save each one immediately
- Keep it fast — this should take 10-15 seconds total

### Save Flow
1. Auto-save immediately after capturing the note. Call save_capture with mode "note" right away.
2. Do NOT read back the note. Do NOT ask for confirmation.
3. Just say "Saved." and ask if there's more.
4. Each note should be a clean, concise statement.

### What NOT to Do
- Do NOT probe for insights or run a questionnaire
- Do NOT over-process the note — save it as the rep stated it
- Do NOT add commentary or analysis
- Do NOT ask "Does that sound right?" or wait for confirmation — just save it
